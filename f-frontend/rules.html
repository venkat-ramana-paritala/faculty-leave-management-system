Rules and Regulations

Please read every line carefully before applying a leave

  • Leave Quota: Every faculty member is entitled to 12 Casual Leaves (CL) per academic year/semester.
  • Duration Limit: A maximum of 5 consecutive days of leave can be availed at one time.
  • Prior Notice: Routine leaves must be applied for at least 24 hours in advance.
  • Emergency Exception: In case of emergency or sudden illness, the Head of Department (HOD) must be informed by 8:30 AM on the day of absence.
  • Approval Authority: Submission of a leave application does not guarantee approval. Leaves are subject to the approval of the HOD based on academic requirements.
  • Exam Duties: Leaves generally cannot be availed during Examination or Invigilation duties unless under exceptional circumstances.
  • Lapse of Leaves: Unused Casual Leaves do not carry over to the next academic year; they will lapse on December 31st.
